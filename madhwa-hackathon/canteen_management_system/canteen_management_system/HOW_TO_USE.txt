============================================================
   CanteenAI - Simple Prediction Dashboard
============================================================

HOW TO START:
-------------
1. Double-click: START.bat
2. Open browser: http://localhost:5000

WHAT YOU GET:
-------------
Two prediction windows:
- Next Day Prediction (tomorrow's forecast)
- Weekly Forecast (7-day predictions)

HOW TO USE:
-----------
1. Click "Generate Prediction" for next day
2. Click "Generate 7-Day Forecast" for weekly
3. View results in tables below buttons

THAT'S IT!
----------
Simple, clean, just predictions.

To stop: Press Ctrl+C in the terminal window
